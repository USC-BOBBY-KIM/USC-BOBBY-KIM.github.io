About Me

Hello! I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. My research interests lie in multimodal artificial intelligence, audio and speech processing, drone detection, knowledge graphs, and trustworthy language models.

Previously, I worked as an AI Researcher at the AI R&D Center of the Korea Military Academy, where I developed AI-powered surveillance systems, battlefield-noise robust speech recognition models, and user-friendly object detection systems. I also collaborated with Purdue University on drone-to-drone detection and self-supervised acoustic drone detection.
